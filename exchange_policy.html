Exchange Policy

Our exchange policy is designed to offer flexibility and customer satisfaction. If, for any reason, you're not completely satisfied with your shoe purchase, we gladly accept exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Whether you need a different size or color, our hassle-free process ensures a smooth transition. The exchanged item must be in unworn condition with original packaging intact. To initiate an exchange, customers can contact our dedicated support team, who will guide them through the simple process. We are committed to ensuring that every customer finds the perfect fit, and our exchange policy reflects this commitment to a seamless and gratifying shopping experience.
